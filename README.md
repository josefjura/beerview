# beerview

> Zero-effort tap list publishing for small craft beer pubs.

Most craft beer pub owners are passionate about beer, not about maintaining websites or social media. Beerview is built around that reality: prepare your upcoming kegs in advance, then tap a single button when you put one on — the rest happens automatically.

## The problem

Small craft beer pubs in Prague (and elsewhere) keep their tap lists on a chalkboard. The most diligent ones post to Facebook manually every afternoon. Nobody has a reliably updated online tap list. Customers have to physically show up to know what's pouring.

## The solution

A feather-light app with one core workflow:

1. **Queue** — Add upcoming kegs during quiet prep time (search beer by name, pull details automatically)
2. **Switch** — When a keg goes on tap, press one button. No typing, no copy-pasting
3. **Publish** — The tap list updates instantly: public URL, embeddable widget for their homepage, automatic Facebook/Instagram post

## Goals

- Minimise owner work during service hours to near zero
- No learning curve — if you can use a phone, you can use beerview
- Give small pubs the same online presence as large venues, for free
- Give beer hunters in Prague (and beyond) accurate, real-time tap lists

## Non-goals

- Not a competitor to Untappd for Business (different audience — pubs that would never sign up for a SaaS platform)
- Not a check-in / rating app (Untappd already does this well)
- Not a commercial product — this is a gift to the local craft beer community
